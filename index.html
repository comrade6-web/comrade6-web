What is Comrade6? +

Comrade6 is a 100% decentralized on-chain matrix protocol on the Polygon Network. It operates autonomously without any central admin. Every transaction is a direct peer-to-peer distribution executed instantly by the smart contract.

Distribution Rule: How It Works +

The protocol follow a strictly coded algorithm to distribute contributions. When a member joins, the smart contract instantly calculates and triggers distributions to the qualified upline wallets in real-time.
